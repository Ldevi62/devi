Mathematics Aptitude Test

The Mathematics Aptitude Test is designed to evaluate your basic mathematical skills, logical reasoning ability, and problem-solving approach. This test consists of 10 multiple-choice questions, each carrying 1 mark. You will have 10 minutes to complete the test.

Topics covered include percentages, basic algebra, square roots, geometry, arithmetic operations, prime numbers, exponents, and averages. It is suitable for students, job applicants, and anyone looking to assess their mathematical aptitude.

Make sure you have a stable internet connection and a quiet environment before starting the test. Once you begin, the timer will start and cannot be paused. Your score will be displayed immediately after submission.
